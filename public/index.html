Amazing Features

Open Chatting Matches

You can chat openly with other PSL Fans in any of current day's matches. Open Chat Messages.

Team Chatting with Favorite Followers

When you sign up, you will select your favorite PSL team and can chat with other users having same favorite team.

Matches Details

You can check today's match details like time, venue, number of users joined the chat and total number of messages sent to this chat.

Messages Reporting System

Message Reporting System: One can report any abusive/vulgar message. We will take a look at that content and block that user for next 24 hours.

Authentic Users for Chatting

You can read other users messages openly but if you want to send a message, you need to sign up within the application.

Login System

Login to start chatting with other PSL Fans.

How it works

PSL Fans is designed for cricket fans from all around the world who follows Pakistan Super League. It enables users to chat with other people on the current day matches publicly. You can share your views about the playing teams and players and let other people know about it. Users can select their favorite team and can chat with other people having same favorite team. Every chat message sent from your account will be public for other users. Users of PSL fans from all around the world can read your messages but only logged in users can send messages. Unethical content can be reported and we will block that user for 24 hours. You can invite your friends too, so they can participate with you for having more fun.
